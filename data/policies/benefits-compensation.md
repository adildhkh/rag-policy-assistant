# Employee Benefits & Compensation Policy
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026
**Applies To:** All eligible employees
**Company Context:** Mid-size technology company (200–500 employees)

## 1. Purpose and Philosophy
The Company’s compensation and benefits program is designed to attract, retain, and motivate high-performing employees while remaining competitive within the technology industry. Our approach balances market-based compensation, performance incentives, and comprehensive benefits that support employees’ health, financial security, and overall well-being.
This policy outlines the structure, eligibility, and administration of employee compensation and benefits. All benefits are subject to applicable laws and plan documents.

## 2. Eligibility Overview
**Full-time employees** (working 30+ hours per week) are eligible for all benefits described in this policy.
**Part-time employees** may be eligible for select benefits on a prorated basis.
**Contractors and interns** are not eligible unless explicitly stated in their agreement.
Benefits generally become effective on the **first day of the month following the employee’s start date**.

## 3. Health Insurance Benefits
The Company offers a comprehensive health insurance program, including medical, dental, and vision coverage.
### 3.1 Medical Insurance
Employees may choose from the following plans:
**Monthly Employee Contribution (Employee Only):** - PPO Standard: USD 120 - PPO Premium: USD 180 - HDHP: USD 90
Dependent coverage is available at additional cost.
### 3.2 Dental Insurance
Preventive care covered at **100%**
Basic services covered at **80%**
Major services covered at **50%**
Employee contribution: **USD 20/month (employee only)**
### 3.3 Vision Insurance
Annual eye exam: Covered at 100%
Frames or contact lenses: Up to USD 150 allowance
Employee contribution: **USD 8/month**

## 4. Retirement Benefits (401(k))
### 4.1 Employee Contributions
Employees may contribute up to the maximum allowed by law on a pre-tax or Roth basis.
### 4.2 Company Match
The Company matches: - **100% of the first 4%** of employee contributions - **50% of the next 2%**
Maximum Company match: **5% of base salary**

## 5. Life and Disability Insurance
### 5.1 Life Insurance
Basic life insurance equal to **1× annual base salary** (Company-paid)
Optional supplemental life insurance available at employee cost
### 5.2 Disability Insurance
Short-Term Disability: Up to **60% of base salary** for 12 weeks
Long-Term Disability: Up to **60% of base salary** after 90 days

## 6. Performance Bonus Program
### 6.1 Annual Bonuses
Eligible employees may receive an annual performance bonus based on individual, team, and Company performance.
### 6.2 Quarterly Incentives
Certain roles (Sales, Customer Success) may participate in quarterly incentive plans tied to measurable KPIs.

## 7. Equity and Stock Options
### 7.1 Eligibility
Equity awards may be granted to: - Senior engineers and technical specialists - Managers and executives - High-impact hires
### 7.2 Vesting Structure
Standard **4-year vesting** with a **1-year cliff**
Vesting occurs monthly after the cliff
Equity awards are governed by the Company’s equity plan documents.

## 8. Wellness and Gym Reimbursement
The Company supports employee wellness through:
Gym or fitness reimbursement up to **USD 40/month**
Eligible expenses include gym memberships, fitness classes, or wellness apps
Receipts are required for reimbursement.

## 9. Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
The Company provides a confidential EAP at no cost to employees and their immediate family members.
Services include: - Mental health counseling - Financial and legal consultations - Stress management and wellness resources
Up to **6 free counseling sessions per issue per year** are provided.
